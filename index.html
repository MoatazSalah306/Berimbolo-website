SECURITY ADVICE

Strengthen your entry points

Ensure all doors have deadbolts with at least a 1-inch throw and reinforce door jambs if necessary.

Deter with visibility

Install visible security cameras and yard signs announcing your security system..

Layer your security

A single security measure is better than none, but a combination offers the strongest defense.

Create a routine that discourages opportunists

Use timers to switch on lights inside the house in the evenings and invest in smart plugs to control electronics remotely.

Be mindful of social media

Avoid sharing vacation plans or details about being away from home on public platforms.
